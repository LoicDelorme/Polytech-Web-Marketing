PART 1: GENERAL USE


1. Basic FrenchCoin Services.

1.1. Eligibility. To be eligible to use the FrenchCoin Services, you must be at least 18 years old.

Your eligibility to access certain FrenchCoin Services also depends on the country in which you reside. The list of FrenchCoin Services accessible by country can be found at https://www.frenchcoin.fr/global.

1.2. FrenchCoin Services. Your FrenchCoin account ("FrenchCoin Account") encompasses the following basic FrenchCoin services: One or more hosted Digital Currency wallets that allow users to store certain supported digital currencies, like Bitcoin or Ethereum ("Digital Currency"), and to track, transfer, and manage their supported Digital Currencies (the "Hosted Digital Currency Wallet"); Digital Currency conversion services through which users can buy and sell supported Digital Currencies in transactions with FrenchCoin (the "Conversion Services"); and a Fiat Currency (e.g. GBP, EUR, CAD) account for use in connection with other FrenchCoin Services (a "Currency Wallet") and for eligible users, a Digital Currency exchange platform ("GDAX") (collectively the "FrenchCoin Services"). The risk of loss in trading or holding Digital Currency can be substantial. You should therefore carefully consider whether trading or holding Digital Currency is suitable for you in light of your financial condition.

3. Hosted Digital Currency Wallet.

3.1. In General. The Digital Currencies that FrenchCoin supports may change from time to time. If you have any questions about which Digital Currencies FrenchCoin currently supports, please visit https://support.frenchcoin.fr. 3.2. Digital Currency Transactions. FrenchCoin processes supported Digital Currency according to the instructions received from its users and we do not guarantee the identity of any user, receiver, requestee or other party. You should verify all transaction information prior to submitting instructions to FrenchCoin. In the event you initiate a Digital Currency Transaction by entering the recipient's email address and the recipient does not have an existing FrenchCoin Account, FrenchCoin will email the recipient and invite them to open a FrenchCoin Account. If the designated recipient does not open a FrenchCoin Account within 30 days, FrenchCoin will return the supported Digital Currency associated with the transaction to your FrenchCoin Account. Once submitted to a Digital Currency network, a Digital Currency Transaction will be unconfirmed for a period of time pending sufficient confirmation of the transaction by the Digital Currency network. A transaction is not complete while it is in a pending state. Funds associated with transactions that are in a pending state will be designated accordingly, and will not be included in your FrenchCoin Account balance or be available to conduct transactions. FrenchCoin may charge network fees (miner fees) to process a Digital Currency transaction on your behalf. FrenchCoin will calculate the network fee in its discretion, although FrenchCoin will always notify you of the network fee at or before the time you authorize the transaction.

4. Conversion Services.

4.1. In General. Eligible users in certain jurisdictions may buy or sell supported Digital Currency through the Conversion Services. The Conversion Services are subject to the FrenchCoin "Conversion Rate" for the given transaction. "Conversion Rate" means the price of a given supported Digital Currency amount in terms of Fiat Currency or other Digital Currency as quoted on the FrenchCoin Site. The Conversion Rate is stated either as a "Buy Price" or as a "Sell Price," which is the price in terms of Fiat Currency or Digital Currency at which you may buy or sell supported Digital Currency to FrenchCoin. You acknowledge that the Buy Price Conversion Rate may not be the same as the Sell Price Conversion Rate at any given time, and that FrenchCoin may add a margin or “spread” to the quoted Conversion Rate. You agree, as a condition of using any FrenchCoin Conversion Services, to accept the Conversion Rate as the sole conversion metric. FrenchCoin reserves the right to delay any Conversion Service transaction if it perceives a risk of fraud or illegal activity. FrenchCoin does not guarantee the availability of its Conversion Service, and the act of purchasing supported Digital Currency from FrenchCoin does not result in a guarantee that you may sell your supported Digital Currency to FrenchCoin.

You authorize FrenchCoin to initiate debits from your selected payment method(s) in settlement of purchase transactions. A Conversion Fee (defined below) applies to all purchase transactions. Although FrenchCoin will attempt to deliver supported Digital Currency to you as promptly as possible, funds may be debited from your selected payment method before Digital Currency is delivered to your FrenchCoin Account. We will make best efforts to fulfill all transactions, but in the rare circumstance where FrenchCoin cannot fulfill your purchase order, we will notify you and seek your approval to fulfill the purchase order at the contemporaneous Buy Price Conversion Rate. To secure the performance of your obligations under this Agreement, you grant to FrenchCoin a lien on and security interest in and to the balances in your account.

4.3. Sale Transactions. After successfully completing the Verification Procedures, you may sell supported Digital Currency by linking a valid payment method. You authorize FrenchCoin to debit your FrenchCoin Account(s) and initiate payments to your selected payment method(s) in settlement of sell transactions. An applicable Conversion Fee (defined below) applies to all sale transactions. Your receipt of funds will depend on the payment type, and may take up to three or more business days.

The applicable Conversion Fee is displayed to you on the FrenchCoin Site prior to you completing a Conversion Service transaction. FrenchCoin will not process a conversion transaction if the Conversion Fee and any other associated fees, such as wire transfer fees, would exceed the value of your transaction.
